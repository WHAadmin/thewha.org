NOGWHISTO Members

The World History Association is a member of the Network of Global and World History Organizations (NOGWHISTO), along with other similar organizations around the world:

  • African Network in Global History / Réseau Africain d’Histoire Mondiale ( ANGH / RAHM )
    Contact: President Peter Adebayo <peter_adebayo@yahoo.com>
  • Asian Association of World Historians ( AAWH )
  • European Network in Universal and Global History ( ENIUGH )
  • International Big History Association ( IBHA )
